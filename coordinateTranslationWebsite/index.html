About the Research

This website is meant to explain the basics of what a reference frame is in 2-D space and eventually it will cover representations of the following rotations in 3-D space:

  • Euler Angles
  • Yaw-Pitch-Roll
  • Direction Cosine Matrix
  • Axis-angle and Rodrigues Parameters
  • Quaternions

About the Staff

Dr. Clark Taylor has been an Assistant Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Air Force Institute of Technology since 2018. He holds a Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering (Computer Engineering) from the University of California where he graduated in 2004. He has provided research on the topic of coordinate translations which is discussed throghout this website.



Shawn Ruby is an undergraduate student who is interning at the ANT center within the Air Force Institute of Technology. He is currently studying for a Computer Science degree, and because of that, he has been responsible for the website and web app development portions of this project.
